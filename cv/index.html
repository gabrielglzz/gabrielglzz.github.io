Summary Researcher specializing in High-Performance Computing (HPC) interconnection networks. My work focuses on the implementation of hybrid topologies (KNS), congestion control mechanisms based on fine-grain monitoring, and the simulation of exascale architectures (BXI, RISC-V). I hold a Ph.D. from the University of Castilla-La Mancha, with international research experience at CERN (Switzerland) and FORTH (Greece).
